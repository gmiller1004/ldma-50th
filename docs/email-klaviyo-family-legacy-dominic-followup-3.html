The Companion Add-On is an optional lifetime upgrade for LDMA members. It names an eligible family member on your account so they can use LDMA facilities without you present for every check-in.

What Companion includes

✓  Named companion on your LDMA membership record
✓  Independent check-in at LDMA private campgrounds
✓  Access to LDMA patented claims for prospecting on their schedule
✓  Travel and camp when your calendars do not align
✓  Lifetime add-on — one-time purchase tied to your membership
Who can be named as Companion
One eligible person per membership, typically:
Spouse · Adult child (18+) · Parent · Grandparent

Without Companion

Family may need the primary member present to check in or use camps and claims on the membership.

With Companion

Named family member has their own access line and can use LDMA camps and claims independently.

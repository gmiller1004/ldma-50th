Membership here is more than access to ground. It is an investment in a way of life your family can inherit.

In 1976, George “Buzzard” Massie and a small group of prospectors gathered at Italian Bar with a simple idea: gold, grit, and brotherhood on real mining ground. Fifty years later, that idea spans private campgrounds, patented claims, and generations of members who raised children and grandchildren at camp.

Why we offer legacy add-ons now

Most members never needed to think about transferability or a companion line on their account—until schedules diverged, family grew, or someone asked, “What happens to my membership someday?”

We structured optional add-ons so you can address today’s trips and tomorrow’s handoff without guessing. Companion for independent camp and claim access. Transferability to name an heir. Pre-paid transfer fee so your family is not left with an unexpected cost at a difficult time.

Your account determines which pieces still make sense—and at what value. That is intentional. A one-size-fits-all price would not respect members who already invested in part of the package years ago.
